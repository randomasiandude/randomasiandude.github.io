About Thanh Phat

Thanh Phat is a premium durian distribution company with high standards, focusing on quality over quantity. We take pride in sourcing only the finest durians from trusted growers who share our passion for excellence.

Each durian is carefully selected, handled with utmost care, and shipped with love to ensure that you receive the perfect fruit every time. Our commitment to quality means we reject more durians than we accept, ensuring only the best reach our customers.

We believe that enjoying a durian should be a memorable experience, which is why we pay attention to every detail from tree to table.
